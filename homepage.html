WHAT WE DO
The Work and Travel USA program by JourneyJobs USA gives international students and young adults the opportunity to live, work, and explore the United States. Participants gain hands-on experience in industries like hospitality, retail, and seasonal work, earning money to fund their travels while fully immersing themselves in American culture. From vibrant resorts in California to ski lodges in Colorado, every job offers a unique and rewarding

JourneyJobs USA provides full support, guiding you through the visa process, helping with job placement, and offering 24/7 assistance to ensure your journey is smooth and stress-free. Beyondwork, participants can explore iconic destinations and create unforgettable memories. Combining professional growth, cultural exchange, and adventure, JourneyJobs Work and Travel USA program is truly a life-changing experience.
